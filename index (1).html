E-commerce & Social Commerce
Community Buying & Group-Deal Feature

A community-driven group buying feature for LoveLocal/Pincode app that enables neighbourhood customers to pool orders for bulk discounts.

Problem Statement

Many users in the same locality buy similar groceries weekly, but local stores don't leverage group buying or bulk deals.

Consequences

  • Higher prices for consumers due to individual orders
  • Lower order volumes per store
  • Missed loyalty opportunities for customers and merchants
  • Inefficient delivery logistics

Design Goal

Launch a community group-buying feature to bundle neighbourhood orders for discounts while improving store efficiency.

Key Considerations

  • Group deal creation and discovery
  • Minimum order thresholds and dynamic pricing
  • Auto-split delivery coordination
  • Store incentive engine for participation
  • Real-time order tracking for group members
  • Payment splitting and refund management

My Role

Designed and prototyped the community buying experience, conducted user research, and defined feature requirements.

Impact

Reduced product costs via bulk ordering
Increased customer loyalty
Improved store inventory management
Enhanced community engagement

Insurance Claims Orchestration Platform

A unified claims orchestration platform that coordinates the entire claim journey across policyholders, hospitals, TPAs, insurers, and auditors.

Problem Statement

Insurance claims involve policyholders, hospitals, garages, TPAs, insurers, and auditors operating on disconnected systems.

Consequences

  • Long claim settlement times affecting satisfaction
  • High volume of manual follow-ups
  • Lack of transparency in claim status
  • Customer dissatisfaction and churn
  • Increased operational costs

Design Goal

Build a claims orchestration platform that coordinates claim journeys across stakeholders with transparency and efficiency.

Key Considerations

  • Unified claim timeline visible to all parties
  • Automated document ingestion and verification
  • SLA tracking and escalation workflows
  • Fraud risk detection signals
  • Multi-stakeholder notification system
  • Compliance and audit trail management

My Role
